Questions

What architects ask first

Does the layered texture have to show?

No. Corduroy is a choice, and a beautiful one. Walls can also be troweled, coated, or finished conventionally. The texture is a palette, not a constraint.

How early should you be involved?

Schematic design, ideally. Ten minutes against the printable envelope at SD saves weeks at CD. The best printed buildings were designed as printed buildings from the first sketch.

Can we do a small first piece?

Yes, and you should. A landscape wall, a pavilion, a garden moment: a contained scope that lets your office learn the medium on a real deliverable.
