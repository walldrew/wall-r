Technology

What is additive construction manufacturing?

3D concrete printing, 3DCP, is construction by addition. A machine extrudes an engineered mortar in precise layers, straight from the design file. Material goes only where the structure needs it. No forms, no offcuts, no waiting on framing crews.

1The printer

Gantry systems or robotic arms drawing the wall path directly from the model, pass after pass, at millimeter repeatability.

2The material

Printable mortars engineered to hold their shape wet and cure strong, tuned to climate and pump distance.

3The print

Walls rise in continuous beads. Openings and conduit paths print in place instead of being cut in later.

Why it matters

Build more. Waste less. Design free.

Waste

Additive manufacturing uses only what it needs. No formwork to build and strip, no lumber offcuts. Material goes into the wall, not the landfill.

Speed

Wall systems print in days with a crew of two to three, compressing the critical path between slab and roof.

Future proof

Monolithic concrete built for Florida reality: hurricane resistance, termite immunity, fire performance, durability insurers can price.

Design freedom

Curves, tapers, and organic forms print at the same cost as straight lines. Expressive architecture without the formwork premium.
