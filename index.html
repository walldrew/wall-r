The premise

Construction, meet Design

Formless concrete + perfect translation = design intent.

Design is bound by means and methods. They've remained largely unchanged for 70 years... until now. A robot “prints” structural concrete exactly where it’s supposed to be, layer by layer. Curves become possible.

How it works
Technology

What is additive construction manufacturing (3DCP)?

3D concrete printing, 3DCP, is efficiency-optimized construction, using strictly what's needed, nothing more. No wasted labor, no wasted materials, no wasted time.

1The printers

We use only deployable, highly-mobile systems designed for the field

2The material

Several options: redi-mix, commercial offerings, and proprietary. Compression strengths range from 3k to 7.5k

3The print

Every wall goes up the same: Accurately, repeatably, consistently.

Why it matters

Flexible design. Tighter tolerance. No waste.

Waste

Additive manufacturing uses only what it needs. No formwork to build and strip, no lumber offcuts. Material goes into the wall, not the landfill.

Speed

The machine and crew are dedicated to the project until completion.

Future proof

Monolithic concrete built for Florida reality: hurricane resistance, termite immunity, fire performance, durability insurers can price.

Design freedom

Curves, tapers, and organic forms print at nearly the same cost as straight lines. Expressive architecture without the formwork premium.
