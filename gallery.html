Designs & Character

Beautiful Layers The distinctive horizontal bead pattern left by the printer nozzle, pass after pass. No two walls are identical.
Innovative Wall Design Complex geometry that would be prohibitively expensive in conventional formwork, printed at the same cost as a straight wall.
Customizable Patterns Surface texture is a design choice, not a constraint. Corduroy beads, smooth trowel, or anything in between.
Walls with a soul Character built into the material itself. Concrete that carries the memory of the machine and the hand of the operator.
