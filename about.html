Drew Williams, founder of Wallr

Not from construction. Obsessed with it now.

I'm passionate about efficiency, sustainability, and stewardship, and I decided I wanted to make the greatest positive impact I can on this world. So I became a 3D construction printing entrepreneur.

My goal is to get this technology into as many hands as possible, as soon as possible. Additive manufacturing uses only what it needs, and creates stunning forms and features in its wake. I'd love to tell you more about what's possible.
