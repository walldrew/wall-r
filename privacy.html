Privacy Policy

Wallr, LLC — 319 Clematis St, Suite 300, West Palm Beach, FL 33401
Effective date: July 4, 2026

The short version

We collect what you send us through the contact form, plus the minimal technical data any web host records. We use it to answer you and to scope your project. We do not sell personal information, we do not run advertising, and we do not use tracking cookies.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide. When you submit the contact form, we receive: first and last name, email address, the role you select, and your message. Please do not include sensitive personal information in the message field.

Information collected automatically. Our hosting provider (Netlify) records standard technical logs: IP address, browser type, requested pages, and timestamps, used for security and operation of the Site. The Site does not set tracking or advertising cookies and does not run third-party analytics as of the effective date. The calculator runs entirely in your browser; values you enter are not transmitted to us.

Fonts. The Site loads typefaces from Google Fonts. When your browser requests those files, Google receives the technical data inherent in any web request, such as your IP address. See Google's privacy policy for details.

2. How we use your information

We use the information you submit to: respond to your inquiry; evaluate and scope potential projects; prepare proposals; maintain a record of correspondence; and protect the Site against abuse. We do not use your information for advertising and do not send marketing newsletters unless you explicitly ask.

3. How your inquiry travels

  1. Netlify, Inc. hosts the Site and operates the form backend. Your submission is received and forwarded to our email address.
  2. Our email provider receives and stores the forwarded inquiry as ordinary business email. [Insert provider before launch.]
  3. Workflow automation (planned). We may connect an automation service such as Make.com to route project inquiries. Any such tool acts as a service provider and this policy will be updated to name the tool when it is in use.

These providers process personal information on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes. We do not sell or rent personal information to anyone.

4. Retention

We keep inquiry records as long as reasonably needed to handle the inquiry, run the resulting business relationship, and meet legal and accounting obligations.

5. Security

We use reputable providers, restrict access to inquiry data to people who need it, and keep credentials protected. No internet transmission is perfectly secure, but we take protection of your information seriously and limit what we collect in the first place.

6. Your choices and rights

You can contact us at [insert contact email] to: request a copy of personal information we hold about you; ask us to correct it; or ask us to delete it. A plain email is enough.

7. Children

The Site is a business site directed at construction professionals and adults. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

8. Do Not Track

The Site does not track visitors across third-party websites, so there is nothing to turn off in response to Do Not Track signals.

9. Changes to this policy

If our practices change, we will update this policy and its effective date before or at the time the change takes effect.
